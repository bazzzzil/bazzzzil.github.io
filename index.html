This chart was created by recording the daily death toll from Al Jazeera using the Wayback Machine to scrub the website between October 9 and present day.
Notes: The Palestinian death count is widely thought to be underestimated due to to bodies trapped under the rubble as well as underreporting of deaths due to disease, thirst and/or starvation.
Total Israeli death count includes military and police. As of November 11, Israeli sources have confirmed the total deaths to be just under 1,200 (1,152 according to Haaretz), compared with the initial widely circulated number of 1,400.
A complete list is available from Haaretz.
Deaths in the West Bank, also reported here, are due to state-sponsored settler violence and IDF attacks on West Bank protesters.
